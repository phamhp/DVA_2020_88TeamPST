Project Summary

Yelp provides a platform for customers to rate businesses between one and five stars based on their interactions with the businesses. Online reviews can affect Yelp users’ decisions on what restaurant they will go to. Further, usage of Location-Based Social Networks is redefining customer experiences and choices with some of these companies generating huge profits. However, reviews from overly positive or negative reviewers can skew a business’s rating. We propose a method that rates businesses more closely with how future users will rate the business.
